A Modern Data Operating System
for Government Agencies

Scale and simplify your data insights to power IT modernization, deliver better citizen services, and drive innovation.

To Better
Serve and Protect

DataOS enables government agencies to integrate all data from multiple sources into a single point of access. With granular level permission controls, every user has access to the right data for easy insight discovery across their entire data universe. With DataOS, government agencies can quickly and easily turn massive amounts of data into actionable decisions.

Government Agencies Use Cases

Real-Time Data

Unlock comprehensive views into all data for users to leverage real-time insights for more informed decision-making that better serve citizens, without taxing resources.

Data 360

Rein in data from disparate sources for deeper insights and process automations to quickly improve and deliver effective services and communication between agencies and citizens.

Data Sharing

Easily collaborate with other departments, agencies, partners, and citizens with granular security and governance controls.

Governance

Identify and protect sensitive data to comply with strict government regulations. DataOS gives you governance at every point in your data lifecycle, combined with observability to ensure your data is being used safely and securely.
